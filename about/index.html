All about Dr. Watson

The world's [second] best-known detective of all time.

My name is John H. Watson, also known as Dr. Watson.

My story begins when I started collaborating with the acclaimed detective Sherlock Holmes. Together, we solved innumerable cases during a successful relationship of several years.

Many of our cases were remarkably complex and involved notorious people, which got the attention of the media and many fans. We had articles and even books and stories written about us and, especially, about the supposed “genius” of Mr. Holmes… After a decade of our partnership, I became tired of Mr. Holmes' eccentric personality and his problems with drugs and alcohol. That's when I decided to start my own detective agency.

I promised to never again be the sidekick of the show, this became my mantra. Consequently, my team and I are glad to present to you one of the best detective agencies in the world, with a successful rate of 100% of cases solved.
